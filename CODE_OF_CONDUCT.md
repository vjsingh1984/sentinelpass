# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want SentinelPass to be a professional, safe, and inclusive OSS project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in reviews and discussions.
- Focus criticism on code and design, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Share reproducible evidence when reporting issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption, spam, or bad-faith reporting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments/PRs that violate this policy.
Repeated or severe violations can lead to temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to maintainers via repository security/contact channels listed in `SECURITY.md`.
